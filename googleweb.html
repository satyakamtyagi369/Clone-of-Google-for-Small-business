Getting your business online
Time to complete: 14 minutes
"E-commerce” simply means buying and selling products and services online. If you want to accept payments on your website or set up an online store to sell things, it’s time to get started with e-commerce. In this lesson, you’ll learn what e-commerce is, how to start using e-commerce, and two types of e-commerce solutions you can consider.
E-commerce is another name for “selling things online.” Different businesses may use e-commerce in different ways, ranging from accepting payments on a website to creating a full online store.
